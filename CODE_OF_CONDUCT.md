# Code of Conduct

We are committed to a respectful, harassment-free community. Be constructive, assume good intent, respect different experience levels, and focus technical criticism on the work rather than the person.

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, discrimination, threats, doxxing, sexualized conduct, or sustained disruption. Maintainers may remove content or restrict participation when necessary to protect the community.

Report conduct concerns privately to the repository owner through GitHub. Reports will be reviewed promptly and handled as confidentially as practical.
