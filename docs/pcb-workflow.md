# PCB paint-ablation workflow

1. Run EDA DRC and export the complete fabrication package.
2. Verify board outline and copper polarity in the app preview.
3. Confirm every via and plated/non-plated drill count against the EDA output.
4. Clean and degrease the copper, apply an even opaque paint coat, and let it cure.
5. Fix the blank against repeatable registration edges.
6. Frame TOP, confirm bounds, and ablate under continuous supervision.
7. Flip the board exactly as selected in the app, returning it to the same registration edges.
8. Frame BOTTOM and verify drill/pad alignment before ablating.
9. Inspect the mask under magnification and repair pinholes or bridges.
10. Etch, neutralize, remove paint, drill, and manually connect vias as required.

Keep copper dust and etchant away from the laser. Follow the safety data sheets for paint, solvents, and etchant, and provide extraction suitable for the chosen coating.
